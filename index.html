About Us

At Stylette Beauty Salon, beauty meets care. Founded with a passion for bringing out the best in every client, our salon is a place where you can relax, rejuvenate, and reinvent yourself.

Led by a CIDESCO-certified professional, we combine international standards with a personal touch, ensuring every service—whether it's skincare, haircare, or wellness—is done with precision, safety, and expertise.

We believe beauty is not just about appearance, but also about confidence and self-care. That's why we focus on high-quality treatments, premium products, and a welcoming atmosphere that makes you feel at home.

Whether you're here for a quick touch-up, a complete transformation, or a relaxing therapy, Stylette is dedicated to making you look and feel your absolute best.
